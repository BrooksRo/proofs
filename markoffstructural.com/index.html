Welcome to Markoff

Welcome and thank you for visiting our website!

Paul and Sandra Markoff founded MSPCCI in July 1982. After many years in the Termite Control industry, coupled with exposure to new construction, Paul decided it was time to start his own company. He made the decision to include family members, along with friends and former employees, to complete his support system. After 28 years of service Paul and his son, Steve Markoff, and his long time friend/co-worker Stan Miller continue to provide the necessary support and commitment to their loyal clients.

 

Paul’s unique style of “BUSINESS WITH A HANDSHAKE” has paid off for him and his company. His clients have learned to trust his word and those of his representatives. Paul continues, after forty years in this industry, to operate his business in a user-friendly manner.
