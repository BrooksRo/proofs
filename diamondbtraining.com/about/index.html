Diamond B Training Stables

About Us

Arabian champions are born, raised & trained at this wonderful facility. Located in the gorgeous northern Willamette Valley of Oregon, Diamond B Training Stables is surrounded by lush greenery and scenic mountain views. What a fantastic place to train horses every day! Also, the stable is just 45 minutes from the Portland Airport, which allows easy access for visitors.

 

Although Russ Brown didn't start his training career with Arabians, he has become one of the top trainers in the United States. Russ has won multiple U.S. and Canadian National Championships, as well as coaching many youths and amateurs to National titles. He was one of the founders of the Arabian Reining Horse Association, and continues to promote the Arabian breed as good looking, athletic horses that are a pleasure to ride. Well known for turning out Champions in the Reining and Cow Horse divisions, Russ also helps with the Pleasure side of the business as well.

 

Mary Jane Brown was brought up with Arabian horses in her blood! She is a third generation Arabian breeder and credits her wonderful horsey heritage for her success. Mary Jane's accomplishments include numerous National, Scottsdale, and Regional Championships in the Open, Amateur and Youth divisions. She loves helping others reach their goals with the horses she so lovingly cares after! Mary Jane is truly a leader and promoter of the Arabian breed, as you can see through her generous gifts of time to the following organizations:

 

Current positions:

  • - Arabian Horse Association Board of Directors, representing Region 4
  • - Arabian Professional & Amateur Horsemen's Association, Vice President
  • - Arabian Horse Association National Futurity Commission, Chair
  • - Arabian Horse Association Education/Evaluation Commission, Member
  • - "R" rated judge, Arabian division, since 1986

 

Past positions:

  • - Arabian Reining Horse Association, President
  • - Arabian Horse Association Working Western Committee, Chair
  • - Arabian Professional & Amateur Horsemen's Association, Chair
  • - Arabian Horse Breeders Association of Oregon, Board of Directors

 

Diamond B Training Stables has achieved success through hard work, honesty, and quality care for the horses at their facility. Russ and Mary Jane Brown also pride themselves in offering an Amateur and Youth program that allows the riders to learn top level riding and showing techniques, while having fun! Enjoyment of your horse is their uppermost concern. If you are interested in the ultimate Arabian experience, Diamond B Training Stables invites you to visit!
