Welcome to Bittersweet Arabians. At Bittersweet we believe in breeding for quality. Our mares have some of the finest bloodlines in the world. We breed them to what we believe to be the best stallions that will produce an Arabian horse of excellence. We strive for the best structure, movement and trainable dispositions. We produce Arabian horses that can compete worldwide in halter, sport horse, performance or pleasure.
